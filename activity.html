Boy Scouts

I am currently a life scout in Troop 201 finalizing my eagle rank. Throughout my scouting experience I met many people that have made a positive, profound impact on me. I have gone on many adventures from attending merit badge classes to being deluged by an insurmountable amount of rain during a scout camp. I started boy scouts back in 3rd grade for an unknown reason. To this day I have no idea why I joined boy scouts but I'm sure glad I did.

At times there were hardships when it came to leadership because many of the younger scouts wouldn't listen, but in the end, everything worked out.

Since it would be unhealthy to stay in my house all the time, I try to get exercise via running on my freetime and participating in track.
