Gaming

In this day and age, video games are such commonplace so I hope you're not surprised. Regardless of the fact, I really do love playing video games because I get to explore someone elses story that they created. I like to think of games as play-able story books created by "modern authors". Modern authors is actually just a term I coined because I consider "old authors" as those who express their thoughts and ideas through books. Not to shame or disrespect them, but this is just something I think about.
